Nepal-Singing-Bowl Nepal singing bowls, also known as Himalayan singing bowls, are ancient instruments used for meditation, healing, and spiritual practices. Crafted from a blend of metals, they produce soothing harmonic tones when struck or rubbed with a mallet, promoting relaxation, stress reduction, and mindfulness. These bowls are deeply rooted in Nepalese and Tibetan culture, with each bowl uniquely crafted and believed to possess therapeutic properties for both the body and mind.
